Prime Living Space

When you want to live but you are not worthy of a space of separation, prime living space is for you.

Principles

  1. Be inclusive
  2. Be Respectful
  3. Be Humble
  4. Be Kind
  5. Be kind
  6. Don't forget: intellect is a property

How it works

When you are in your sleeping quarters, you can be yourself. But in the common spaces, it is never polite to look at others. They might not appreciate being looked at, or they might not like it. It is not considered wrong to want to look at other, but it is wrong to actually look at them. We have the sin, not the sinner.

One good example of a place where you don't want to look at others: the bathrooms. Thy are nice and toasty, so that people don't get cold. They smell like soap and they are always clean because you clean them. Bathrooms are for privacy. Do not look at people in bathrooms.

In kitchens, you make your food. You are allowed to talk to others, if they want to talk. As long as you don't look.

Subjects to avoid whn talking to people:

  • Inflammatory subjects
  • The topic of religion or Atheism
  • Music, forbidden music (no music is forbidden, except loud music.)
  • Preferring cats or dogs
  • Things you remember which are nostalgia
  • Ghosts
  • Things you do outside the Prime Living Space
  • Your role or function
  • How much you pay for things (rent+)
  • How much you get paid for chores

Do not knock on any doors, especially door which have colors on the south-east end of the color wheel. If it's an emergency, you can knock on a white door. If there is not white door anywhere you can king, then you can knock on a door from any of the three other quarters of the color wheel. This you must remember ant not break the rule.

Windows

There are no windows that look out to the outside of the Prime Living Space. If you see a window-like object, that's a one-way mirror. If you can see your reflection, that means that you are on the non-see-through side of the one-way mirror. If you can see a room or a corridor through a window, that means people from the other side cannot see you. You can use this time to look at them. Also, be aware that any time you can see a mirror-side of the window, somebody may be looking at you. You can do what you want with that knowledge, but we trust you will remember about the principles and the rules.

Staff will not speak to you or look at you, no matter what you do. That does not mean you will not be punished for breaking the rules. You punishment will come at the hand of a super, and it will come at the appropriate moment. You will not know when the time comes, so be ready.

Door locks

There are door locks, but residents are not given keys. If a door is locked, you cannot cross it until a member of staff unlocks it. Do not ask for a door to be unlocked, that is not polite.

If you find yourself behind a locked door in a single room with no other exit, for example a small sleeping space, you cannot leave until a member of the staff has unlocked the door. Do not knock on the door and say "Hello?", or "Is anybody there?". Nobody wants to hear that. The staff must have had a good reason to lock the door and they will unlock it when appropriate.

Lights out!

If the lights are out, that means it's time to take a nap. If you are near a sleeping space, go there, take up an empty bed. If there are no empty beds there, don't leave and try to look for another one, lay on the floor. If you are far from a sleeping space, just lay down where you are. It's okay to not feel sleepy, just shut your eyes and don't open them until you can see the light through your eyelids.

Dish washing

Do not leave dirty plates in the sink. If you're done with a plate, wash it. If there's no detergent, or no sponge, or the sponge is "yucky", don't worry, if you are more entrepreneurial, you would be living in a better place. Instead of whining, wash the plate in warm water. If there's no warm water, use cold water. If there's no water, use your tongue.

Food

If you are hungry, eat. Do not use any of the food in the cupboards or fridges, unless you put it there. This food is not free for the taking. Bring your food. If you don't have money for food, make money.
